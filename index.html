About Hapoel Holon

Hapoel Holon is a professional basketball team based in Holon, Israel. The team was founded in 1947 and plays in the Israeli Basketball Premier League. Known for their passionate fan base and competitive spirit, Hapoel Holon has won several championships and continues to be a strong contender in Israeli basketball.

Achievements

  • Israeli Basketball Premier League Champions: 2008, 2020
  • Israeli State Cup Winners: 2009, 2018
  • Israeli League Cup Winners: 2018, 2019

Team Roster

The team features a mix of talented local and international players, each bringing unique skills and experience to the court.
